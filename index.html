Stark Special Vehicles

Based in Bangkok and established in 2022, Stark International (Thailand) specializes in the export of a comprehensive range of commercial vehicles, with a focus on popular Toyota and Isuzu Double and Single Cabin Pickup models. Our core expertise includes vehicle customization and body fabrication. We are a trusted manufacturer of specialized vehicles, delivering high-quality FRP (Fibre Reinforced Plastic) Freezer Vans and Trucks, as well as fully equipped Special Ambulances, tailored to meet diverse business needs worldwide.
